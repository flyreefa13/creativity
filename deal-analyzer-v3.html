Cap Rate — Annual NOI ÷ purchase price × 100. Target 6%+ for residential.
Cash-on-Cash — Annual cash flow ÷ cash invested × 100. Target 15%+.
NOI — Net Operating Income. Revenue minus all expenses before mortgage.
DSCR — Debt Service Coverage Ratio. NOI ÷ mortgage payment. Want 1.25+.
SDE — Seller's Discretionary Earnings. Total profit including owner's salary.
ARV — After Repair Value. What property is worth after renovations.
CapEx — Capital Expenditures. Reserve for big repairs (roof, HVAC etc).
PMI — Private Mortgage Insurance. Required on conventional loans under 20% down.
MIP — Mortgage Insurance Premium. FHA's version of PMI. ~0.85%/yr.
LTV — Loan-to-Value. Loan amount ÷ property value × 100.
BRRRR — Buy, Rehab, Rent, Refinance, Repeat. Cash recycling strategy.
1% Rule — Monthly rent should equal ~1% of purchase price.
